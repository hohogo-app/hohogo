# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the Support and FAQ page for HoHoGo. If you can't find the answer you are looking for here, please feel free to contact us at **hohogo.app@gmail.com**.

### 🗺️ General App Usage

**What is HoHoGo?**
It is a gamified travel journal. You can check in to famous landmarks around the world, collect beautifully designed digital badges, unlock themes for your passport, and create a timeline of your global footprint.

**How do I collect a badge?**
To collect a verified badge, you need to "Check In" at a landmark. You can do this in two ways:
1. **Live Check-In:** Be physically near the landmark and use your device's GPS.
2. **Photo Check-In:** Upload a photo taken at the landmark. Our system checks the location data (EXIF) saved inside the photo to verify you were there.

**What is the difference between a "Verified Badge" and a "Travel Memory"?**
*   **Verified Badge:** If you check in while physically at a landmark, or upload a photo with matching GPS data, you earn the official, full-color Badge for that location. It counts toward your Quest progress.
*   **Travel Memory:** If you check in to a landmark but we cannot verify your location (e.g., you are far away and upload a photo without GPS data), it is saved as a "Travel Memory." It will appear in your timeline so you can journal your trip, but you will not receive the official Badge.

### 🔒 Privacy & Data

**How does the Photo Scanner work? Does it upload my camera roll?**
No! The Photo Scanner runs **100% locally on your device**. It scans your camera roll using Apple's local geographic data to find past travels. It will only upload a photo to our servers if you explicitly choose to save it to your Logbook. 

**Why does the app need my Location?**
We use your location solely to verify that you are actually at a landmark when you try to claim a badge. We do not track you in the background. 

**How do I delete my account and data?**
You can delete everything at any time. Go to the **Profile Tab** > tap the **Settings (Gear) icon** > scroll down to the **Danger Zone** and tap **Delete Account**. This will instantly and permanently erase your account, travel timeline, photos, and earned badges from our servers.

### ✈️ Offline Mode & Features

**Can I use the app while traveling without internet or data?**
Yes! We built the app for travelers. If you don't have internet, you can still check in to landmarks, write journal entries, and save photos. The app will securely save your memories offline and automatically upload them the next time you connect to Wi-Fi or cellular data.

**How do I unlock new Themes and Map Icons?**
You can customize your app by inviting friends! Go to the **Passport Tab** > tap the **Gear Icon** > tap **Customize** > and tap **Travel Buddy System**. Share your unique invite code with friends. When they sign up using your code, you will unlock Premium Themes (like Night Owl or Vintage), new Map Vehicles, and exclusive Titles.
